About Us and Our Principles

We guide children in fostering independence and a lifelong love for learning while also gaining respect for themselves, others, and their environment.

We Utilize the Montessori Method

Over a century ago, Dr. Maria Montessori developed a new education movement with the purpose of greatly improving the learning process and outcomes for all students. Palencia Montessori Academy was founded in 2012, where we follow these core principles of the Montessori philosophy in our daily curriculum to provide distinguished, leading educational opportunities for every child in our school.
